# Privacy Policy

## Summary

HedgieMate does not collect, store, or transmit your data. Your Tesla data stays on your TeslaMate server.

## How It Works

- **Direct connection**: HedgieMate connects directly from your device to your TeslaMate API
- **No intermediary**: No data passes through our servers
- **No analytics on your Tesla data**: We don't track your drives, locations, or vehicle information

## What We Don't Collect

- Driving patterns or locations
- Charging history
- Vehicle data
- Personal information
- Usage analytics tied to your Tesla data

## Your Control

- Disconnect anytime by removing the API URL from settings
- All cached data is stored locally on your device
- Delete the app to remove all local data

## Third-Party Services

HedgieMate connects only to:
- Your TeslaMate API (configured by you)
- App Store for purchases and updates
